WHAT IS BIM?

Building information modeling (BIM)

Is a process supported by various tools, technologies and contracts involving the generation and management of digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of places
Step 1

Conceptual design, the first idea of ​​the project to be developed (LOD 200).

Step 2

Preliminary project (we reached a LOD 300 only at the architectural level, 3d images and video of the preliminary project), Real-time corrections of the design.

Step 3

Basic project (we reached a LOD 350 at the level of architecture, structure and facilities.) Management of collision processes, planning and work budget.
